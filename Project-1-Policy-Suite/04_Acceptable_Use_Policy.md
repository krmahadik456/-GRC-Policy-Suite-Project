## 1. Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to define the acceptable and unacceptable use of SecurePay Technologies' information technology (IT) resources. This policy ensures that IT resources are used responsibly, securely, and in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- All employees, contractors, consultants, and temporary workers
- All IT resources owned or managed by SecurePay Technologies
- All devices accessing SecurePay Technologies' network and systems
- Personal devices used for work (BYOD)

---

## 3. General Use Guidelines

### 3.1 Primary Use
- IT resources shall be used primarily for business purposes
- Personal use shall be limited and incidental
- IT resources shall not be used for illegal, unethical, or unauthorized activities

### 3.2 Privacy
- Users shall not expect privacy on company systems
- All activity on company systems may be monitored and logged
- Monitoring shall be conducted in compliance with applicable laws

### 3.3 Data Protection
- Users shall handle data according to its classification (Public, Internal, Confidential, Restricted)
- Sensitive data shall only be transmitted through approved channels
- Users shall not store sensitive data on personal devices without authorization

### 3.4 Security
- Users shall follow all security policies and procedures
- Users shall not share their credentials with anyone
- Users shall report security incidents immediately

---

## 4. Acceptable Uses

### 4.1 Business Activities
- Conducting company business
- Communicating with customers, partners, and colleagues
- Accessing job-related information
- Developing and testing software
- Performing research and analysis

### 4.2 Incidental Personal Use
- Checking personal email (limited)
- Personal phone calls (brief, during breaks)
- Personal appointments (within reason)
- Occasional personal web browsing (during breaks)

### 4.3 Communications
- Professional and respectful communication
- Clear identification when communicating externally
- Use of company email for official correspondence

---

## 5. Prohibited Uses

### 5.1 General Prohibitions

| Activity | Prohibited | Reason |
|----------|------------|--------|
| Accessing illegal content | ❌ | Legal liability |
| Downloading unauthorized software | ❌ | Security risk |
| Sharing passwords | ❌ | Security risk |
| Accessing sensitive data without authorization | ❌ | Privacy violation |
| Using resources for personal gain | ❌ | Conflict of interest |
| Harassment or discrimination | ❌ | Legal and ethical violation |
| Political campaigning | ❌ | Not business-related |
| Excessive personal use | ❌ | Productivity loss |

### 5.2 Email and Messaging

| Activity | Prohibited | Reason |
|----------|------------|--------|
| Sending spam or chain emails | ❌ | Productivity loss |
| Sending confidential data via unsecured email | ❌ | Security risk |
| Impersonating someone else | ❌ | Legal and ethical violation |
| Sending offensive content | ❌ | Legal and ethical violation |
| Using work email for personal business | ❌ | Conflict of interest |
| Forwarding confidential emails without authorization | ❌ | Privacy violation |

### 5.3 Internet and Network

| Activity | Prohibited | Reason |
|----------|------------|--------|
| Accessing adult content | ❌ | Legal and ethical violation |
| Accessing gambling sites | ❌ | Legal and ethical violation |
| Engaging in hacking activities | ❌ | Legal and security risk |
| Downloading copyrighted material | ❌ | Legal liability |
| Bypassing security controls | ❌ | Security risk |
| Using personal VPNs without authorization | ❌ | Security risk |

### 5.4 Social Media

| Activity | Prohibited | Reason |
|----------|------------|--------|
| Sharing confidential company information | ❌ | Security and privacy risk |
| Defaming the company or employees | ❌ | Legal and ethical violation |
| Using company identity for personal social media | ❌ | Misrepresentation |
| Posting from work accounts | ❌ | Unauthorized |

## 6. BYOD (Bring Your Own Device)

### 6.1 Requirements

Personal devices used for work must:
- Have up-to-date anti-virus/anti-malware software
- Be encrypted
- Have screen lock (timeout ≤ 5 minutes)
- Be enrolled in Mobile Device Management (MDM)
- Be updated with the latest security patches

### 6.2 Restrictions

Personal devices shall NOT be used for:
- Storing confidential or restricted data
- Conducting privileged administrative tasks
- Accessing highly sensitive systems

### 6.3 Rights

SecurePay Technologies reserves the right to:
- Remotely wipe company data from personal devices
- Audit devices for compliance
- Deny access from non-compliant devices

---

## 7. Monitoring and Enforcement

### 7.1 Monitoring

SecurePay Technologies shall monitor IT resources for:
- Security violations
- Policy violations
- Performance and capacity issues
- Unauthorized access attempts
- Data exfiltration attempts

### 7.2 Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in:
- Disciplinary action, up to and including termination
- Suspension of IT access
- Legal action where applicable
- Personal liability for damages
